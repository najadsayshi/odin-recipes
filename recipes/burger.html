Chicken Burger

Description

A chicken burger is a juicy and flavorful sandwich made with a seasoned chicken patty, fresh vegetables, and creamy sauces, all placed between soft burger buns.

It’s an easy and popular fast-food style dish enjoyed worldwide, loved for its crisp texture, tender meat, and customizable toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts (or minced chicken)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika or chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato slices, onion rings
  • Mayonnaise or burger sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare patty mixture: Combine chicken (minced or chopped in processor), onion, garlic, egg, breadcrumbs, pepper, paprika, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Shape patties: Divide the mixture into equal portions and flatten them into round burger patties.
  3. Cook patties: Heat a bit of oil in a pan. Cook patties for 4–5 minutes per side until golden and fully cooked through.
  4. Toast buns: Lightly toast the burger buns on a pan or grill for extra flavor.
  5. Assemble: Spread sauce on buns, add lettuce, place the chicken patty, then add tomato and onion. Top with more sauce if desired.
  6. Serve: Close the bun, secure with a toothpick if needed, and serve with fries or a drink.
